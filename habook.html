BACKGROUND

This was assigned as our first group project in my UX / UI course. For this project, we focused on using a variety of UX skills to solve an everyday problem people face. Our group was wondering what problem we wanted to help solve since there are so many out there, but we all agreed that we all had a hard time with our own self-improvement.

CHALLENGES

Working on this project, one of our biggest challenges was gathering the correct data we needed to progress in the right direction. We had to go back and re-assess what it was that we were trying to solve through our app.

THE PROBLEM

Majority of people want to form new habits to improve their quality of life. However, people find themselves having trouble maintaining their habits because they lose motivation throughout the process.

THE SOLUTION

HaBook will help our users achieve their goals by reminding them of their progress to keep them motivated until they have successfully formed their habit.
